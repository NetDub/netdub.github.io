About

In the simplest sense, NetDUB is about spending a bit of time each quarter, socialising with your fellow Networking professionals. To be more complex about it, the plan behind NetMcr was to fill a void in Dublin’s social calendar. Whilst the majority of ‘meetings’ in the city centre on development, we’re focusing specifically on Network Engineers, Architects, Technicians, or perhaps just anyone interested in the profession. The NetDUB events are currently being bootstrapped, and currently looking for a reoccuring venue.
